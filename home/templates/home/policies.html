Cookie Policy

At Nerd Shop, we use cookies on our website to improve your browsing experience and to better understand how our website is used.

A cookie is a small text file that is placed on your device (computer, phone or tablet) when you visit our website. Cookies can help us to recognize your device the next time you visit, provide you with a personalized experience on our website, remember your preferences and settings, and analyze how our website is used.

There are different types of cookies, including:

  • Session cookies - these are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser or leave our website.
  • Persistent cookies - these cookies remain on your device for a longer period of time, even after you close your browser or leave our website. They can be used to remember your preferences and settings, or to provide you with personalized content.
  • Third-party cookies - these cookies are placed by third-party websites or services, such as advertising networks or social media platforms. They are used to track your browsing activity across multiple websites and to display personalized ads.

By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this Cookie Policy. You can control and manage cookies in various ways, including by adjusting your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.

If you have any questions or concerns about our use of cookies, please contact us at contact@nerdshop.com.
